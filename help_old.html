📘 How to Use Viraj Masale
Home Products Shop Contact Login Register About Us 📍 Location

1️⃣ Register

Go to the Registration page and enter your full details. A unique Customer ID will be generated for you.

2️⃣ Login

Use your Customer ID and password to log in securely. You’ll be redirected to the main site after login.

3️⃣ Browse Products

Explore the Shop or Products pages. See prices, ingredients, and select your favorite items.

4️⃣ Add to Cart & Confirm

Add products to your cart. Review your order and click Confirm Order to send the summary via WhatsApp.

5️⃣ Forgot Password?

If you forget your password, use the Forgot Password link to reset it with your Customer ID.

6️⃣ Quick Order

Need fast ordering? Use the Quick WhatsApp Order page to place an order without login.

7️⃣ Your Profile Badge

After login, your profile image and ID will appear at the top-right of every page – like Instagram/Facebook.

📦 Order Summary

Once ordered, you'll be redirected to the Order Details page to see your delivery time and summary.
